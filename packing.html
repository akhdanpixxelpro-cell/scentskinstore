Thoughtful Packaging

At Essentia, we believe that the experience begins long before you smell the scent. Our packaging is a blend of minimal aesthetics and environmental responsibility.

The Classic Box

Designed with a restricted monochromatic palette, our signature boxes are inspired by botanical illustrations and classic typography.

Every box is made from 100% recycled paper, ensuring that beauty doesn't come at the cost of nature.

Amber Glass Vials

We use high-quality amber glass to protect our plant-based essential oils from UV light, preserving the potency and longevity of your scent.

Once empty, these vials are designed to be reused as mini vases or recycled infinitely.

Sustainability Matters

Recycled Paper

FSC-certified paper sourced from responsibly managed forests.

Soy-Based Ink

Biodegradable inks that are safer for the environment during recycling.

No Plastic

We use paper-based tape and honeycomb wrap instead of bubble wrap.
