Crafted by Nature

Transparency is at the heart of everything we do. From the first sprout to the final bottle, discover how we create our signature scents.

01

Sourcing Ingredients

We travel to small, sustainable farms around the globe to source the highest quality botanical ingredients. No synthetic fertilizers, just pure nature.

Our commitment to organic farming ensures that every drop of essential oil is as potent as the plant it came from.

02

Cold-Press Distillation

To preserve the delicate aromatic compounds, we use slow, cold-press distillation techniques. This process takes longer but results in a cleaner, more vibrant scent.

Unlike heat-based methods, our process keeps the therapeutic benefits of the plants intact.

03

Small Batch Blending

We don't believe in mass production. Every bottle of Essentia is blended in small batches in our Los Angeles studio to ensure absolute precision.

Each batch is tested for clarity, scent profile, and skin-safety before it ever reaches our shelves.
